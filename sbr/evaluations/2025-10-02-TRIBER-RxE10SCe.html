 COMMENTS: NOTE: This result has been updated to reflect changes made to some variants for 2025. However all conclusions relevant to the grade remain unchanged. From the 2024 Triber's owner's manual: The 6 graphic is displayed on the in- strument panel in below two states This informs the driver fastening status of each of the rear seat belts every time: – the ignition is switched on; – Status of fastening or unfastening of a rear seat belt. Understanding the graphic 6 : – indicator filled: seatbelt fastened; – indicator unfilled: seatbelt unfastened; Depending on the vehicle the 6 symbol appears when the ignition is switched ON if a rear seat belt is buckled state to unbuckled state or any state change. When the vehicle speed is less than approximately 12 mph (20 km/h) the 6 symbol appears for at least 65 seconds. When the vehicle speed reaches or exceeds 12 mph (20 km/h) if one of the rear seat belts is or becomes un- fastened during the journey: 2 – a beep is emitted for 35 seconds ; and – the 6 symbol is displayed for at least 65 seconds and the symbol for the seat concerned changes to unfilled. The Renault Triber was updated for 2025 and variants with the 17.78 cm TFT instrument cluster have a different visual signal than variants with an LCD panel. However all conclusions relevant to the grade remain. The following additional information is available in the 2025 Triber's owner's manual for these variants: Understanding the graphic 7 : – indicator green: seatbelt fastened; – indicator red: seatbelt unfastened. Depending on the vehicle the 7 sym- bol appears when the ignition is switched ON if a rear seat belt is buckled state to unbuckled state or any state change. When the vehicle speed is less than approximately 20 km/h (12 mph) the 7 symbol appears for at least 65 seconds. When the vehicle speed reaches or exceeds 20 km/h (12 mph) if one of the rear seat belts is or becomes un- fastened during the journey: – a beep is emitted for 35 seconds; and – the 7 symbol is displayed for at least 65 seconds and the symbol for the seat concerned changes to red.
