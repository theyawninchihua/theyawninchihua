 COMMENTS: Multiple media reviews for the Altroz reveal that both rear seatbelt reminder signals activate even when the rear seats are unoccupied. From Autocar India (https://www.autocarindia.com/car-reviews/2025-tata-altroz-facelift-review-sharp-suiter-435512): Irritatingly the rear seats lack load sensors and buzz a seatbelt reminder even if the seats are not occupied. From Rush Lane (https://www.rushlane.com/tata-altroz-facelift-first-drive-review-step-in-the-right-direction-12523592.html): One glaring omission is load sensors on seats that trigger rear seat belt alarm even though there’s no one on these seats. From News Nine (https://www.news9live.com/auto/tata-altroz-2025-facelift-the-good-the-bad-and-everything-in-between-2859129): One of the issues that was irritating was that the rear seatbelt warning kept going off as it lacked load sensors at the backseat.
