 COMMENTS: From the 2024 Kiger's owner's manual: Rear seat belt reminder 8 (Depending on the Vehicle) The 8 graphic is displayed on the in- strument panel in below two states This informs the driver fastening status of each of the rear seat belts every time: – the ignition is switched on; – Status of fastening or unfastening of a rear seat belt. Understanding the graphic 8 : – indicator green: seatbelt fastened; – indicator red: seatbelt unfastened; Depending on the vehicle the 8 sym- bol appears when the ignition is switched ON if a rear seat belt is buckled state to unbuckled state or any state change. When the vehicle speed is less than approximately 12 mph (20 km/h) the 8 symbol appears for at least 65 seconds. When the vehicle speed reaches or exceeds 12 mph (20 km/h) if one of the rear seat belts is or becomes un- fastened during the journey: – a beep is emitted for 35 seconds; and – the 8 symbol is displayed for at least 65 seconds and the symbol for the seat concerned changes to red.
