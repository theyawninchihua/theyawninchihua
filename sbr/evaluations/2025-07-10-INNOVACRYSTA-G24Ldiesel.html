 COMMENTS: From the 2nd-gen Innova's global owner's manual: Rear passengers’ seat belt reminder lights (warning buzzer)*8 (if equipped) Warn the rear passengers to fasten their seat belts → Fasten the seat belt. *8: The rear passengers’ seat belt buzzer sounds to alert the rear passenger that his or her seat belt is not fastened. The buzzer sounds intermittently for 30 seconds after the seat belt is fastened and unfastened and the vehicle reaches a speed of 20 km/h (12 mph). Then if the seat belt is still unfastened the buzzer will sound in a different tone for 90 more seconds.
