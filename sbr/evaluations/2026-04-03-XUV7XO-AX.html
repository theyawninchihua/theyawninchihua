REASON FOR SELECTION: Not evaluated before. The XUV 7XO is a new model.
COMMENTS: From The XUV 7XO's owners' manual: 2.4.7 Passenger Occupant Detection System (PODS) & Seat Belt Reminder Indication: PODS detects the presence of occupant in the front passenger and front facing rear seating locations. If Occupant is present on front passenger and front facing rear passenger seats and not wearing the seatbelt, Seatbelt reminder warning symbol lamp will glow on the center fascia switch provided in vehicle instrument panel and will be continuous until the seatbelt is buckled. It should be noted that the PODS require a minimum weight hence children may not be detected by PODS. Along with this warning symbol, a Chime in DID will be ON for 95 second for front seatbelt reminder and 35 second for front facing rear seatbelt reminder. Do keep in mind that after market seat covers also may deteriorate the detection senor performance, don’t use the non recommended/non authorized seat covers.
