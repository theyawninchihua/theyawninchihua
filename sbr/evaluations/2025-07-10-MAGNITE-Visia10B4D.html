 COMMENTS: From the 2024 Magnite's owner's manual: REAR SEAT BELT REMINDER (if equipped) The rear seat belt reminder appears on the vehicle information display for 65 seconds after the ignition switch is placed in the “ON” posi- tion. If the status of the rear seat belt has been changed the reminder will be displayed again for 65 seconds. When one of the rear seat belts is buckled and becomes unbuckled the corre- sponding rear seat belt indicator will appear. If this occurs while the vehicle speed exceeds 20 km/h (12 MPH) a buzzer will sound for 35 sec- onds.
