Honda's new CITY e:HEV earns FAIL in The Yawning Chihuahua's Rear Seatbelt Reminder Evaluation

31.05.2026



The new Honda CITY e:HEV was launched into the Indian market last week, and today, it has been awarded a FAIL result in the The Yawning Chihuahua Rear Seatbelt Reminder Evaluation.

The new CITY e:HEV brings with it safety updates like i-Size compatibility, but disappointingly, its rear seatbelt reminder behaves as it did when it was added in 2024: it only issues an acoustic signal when any rear seatbelt has been unfastened while driving.

Honda CITY e:HEV
Behaviour of second-level warning in the 2nd-row outboard seats
