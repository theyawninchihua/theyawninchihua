India-spec Kia EV9 fails The Yawning Chihuahua's Rear Seatbelt Reminder Evaluation

11.04.2026



The rear seatbelt reminder (SBR) evaluation for the India-spec Kia EV9 has been published today.

The Kia EV9 arrives in India as an import, but does not behave like some of its overseas versions do, and receives a FAIL result: the India-spec version would fail to notify unbelted occupants in common scenarios. Based on its documentation, the India-spec version's rear seat belt reminder does not have occupant detection, and only issues an audio signal when a rear seatbelt is fastened and then unfastened on the move.

Importantly, this is unlike the behaviour seen in EV9s destined for Europe and Australasia, which have occupant detection in the rear outboard seats, a prerequisite to score seat belt reminder points in those markets' respective consumer test programmes Euro NCAP and ANCAP.
