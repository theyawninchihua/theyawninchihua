Success for MG ZS EV in 'What The Beep?' rear seatbelt reminder evaluation

09.06.2026



The MG ZS EV has today earned a rare PASS result in TYC's What The Beep? seatbelt reminder evaluation.

The page administrator recently had the opportunity to test the ZS EV's rear seatbelt reminder in person, and is pleased to report the system meets the advanced evaluation criteria of What The Beep?.

As evident in the testing video below, the ZS EV's rear seatbelt reminder includes occupant detection, and starts its visual and acoustic signals when an occupant has not buckled up. It does not issue any signal when a seat is unoccupied.
