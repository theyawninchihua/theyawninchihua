COMMENTS: The Honda CITY e:HEV was recently updated. According to its owners' manual:
"Seat Belt Reminder
Rear seats*
Your vehicle monitors rear seat belt use. The driver information interface notifies you about the status of the rear seat belts use.
The display appears when:
• Any of the rear passenger seat belts are unfastened when the power mode is set to ON.
• A rear door is opened and then closed.
• Any of the rear passengers fasten or unfasten their seat belt.
The seat belt reminder indicator blinks and the beeper sounds if any rear passenger seat belts have been unfastened while driving."
